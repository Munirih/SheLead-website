Our Story

SheLead: Women's Leadership and Mentorship Network

The SheLead Team is a dynamic initiative founded during the Women Techsters Bootcamp 4.0 by a group of passionate, like-minded women united by a shared vision of empowerment, collaboration, and leadership.

Rooted in the spirit of mutual support and skill-building, the initiative fosters a thriving community that champions women’s growth in technology by offering mentorship, sharing knowledge, and creating opportunities for leadership and career advancement.

SheLead is a digital platform designed to empower women for a brighter future; to build confidence, gain practical skills and succeed. It empowers women by connecting them to global network of mentors, leadership resources and scholarships, career development tools, supportive community and equity.

Our Mission

Inspiring women to lead boldly through mentorship, opportunity and voice.

  • Foster economic growth by empowering women in the work force
  • Support women leadership development with mentorship and resources
